Overarching Goal: The traditional methods of learning new languages have become outdated and irrelevant. There are foundational problems with the current education structures in place. In the classroom or online applications, students will pour hours of work into learning vocabulary words which are unrealistic to use in everyday situations and in turn easily forgotten. Every person has a different learning style and these lessons are generally standardized and unpersonalized leading to inefficient teaching styles. Even when applications are built to resolve these problems, they tend to lack the grammatical and conversational structure that is required for learning new languages. In conclusion the current problem lies in the irrelevant, impersonal and lack of conversational structure in existing solutions.
